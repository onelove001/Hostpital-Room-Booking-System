Frequently Asked Questions


Joining the gym

Can I try before I buy?

Can I freeze my membership?

You can freeze your membership at any point up to 6 months, however this will extend your contract membership term. All types of memberships can freeze at any time, please call your local site.

How can I re-join?

You can simply re-join us at Places Gym by joining online or calling your local site. You may need to pay a joining fee.

Is there free parking?

There is free parking directly outside Places Gym Sheffield & Preston. Members at Places Gym Hinckley qualify for 2 hours free parking at the Sainsbury's upon receipt of a validated ticket from our gym reception

Are there any changing facilities and showers?

There are changing facilities and showers available at all Places Gym sites.

What if I need help while I'm in the gym?

There will always be a member of the fitness team on the gym floor available to assist or answer any questions you might have.

How do I book my induction?

To book your induction, please call your local site to arrange a suitable date and time.
What benefits will I get if I refer a friend?

Members who refer a friend will receive a free month, that means if you refer 12 friends you will get your gym membership free for a year!
